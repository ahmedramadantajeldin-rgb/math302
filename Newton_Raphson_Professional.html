§ 01

Definition & Core Idea

What Is It?

The Newton-Raphson Method is an iterative root-finding algorithm. Given a differentiable function \(f(x)\), it finds an approximation to a solution of \(f(x)=0\) by repeatedly refining a guess using the function's tangent line.

الفكرة الأساسية: بدل ما نحل المعادلة مباشرةً، نبدأ بتخمين أوّلي ونحسّنه خطوة بخطوة باستخدام المماس.
Geometric Interpretation

At a point \(x_{n-1}\) on the curve, draw the tangent line to \(f\). Where that tangent crosses the \(x\)-axis is the next approximation \(x_n\). Each tangent "overshoots" closer to the true root.

Intuition
Tangent line ≈ local linear model of \(f\). Its zero is a better root estimate.
Speed
Converges quadratically — correct digits double each iteration near the root.
يعني إننا بنرسم خط المماس عند النقطة الحالية، ونشوف وين بيقطع المحور السيني — هاد بيكون تقريبنا الجديد.
§ 02

Formula Derivation

Step-by-Step Derivation
1
The tangent line to \(f\) at the point \(\bigl(x_{n-1},\, f(x_{n-1})\bigr)\) is:
\[ y - f(x_{n-1}) \;=\; f'(x_{n-1})\cdot\bigl(x - x_{n-1}\bigr) \]
هاد هو معادلة خط المماس عند النقطة \(x_{n-1}\).
2
Set \(y = 0\) (find where the tangent meets the \(x\)-axis):
\[ -f(x_{n-1}) \;=\; f'(x_{n-1})\cdot\bigl(x_n - x_{n-1}\bigr) \]
3
Solve for \(x_n\):
\[ x_n - x_{n-1} \;=\; -\frac{f(x_{n-1})}{f'(x_{n-1})} \]
يعني هنا بنستخدم الاشتقاق لنقسم عليه ونعزل \(x_n\).
4
Newton-Raphson Iteration Formula:
\[ \boxed{x_n \;=\; x_{n-1} - \frac{f(x_{n-1})}{f'(x_{n-1})}} \]
with the stopping condition \(f'(x) \neq 0\) at every step.
§ 03

Applicability Conditions

Required Conditions

Before applying the method on an interval \([a, b]\), check:

Condition 1 — Non-zero Derivative
\(f'(x) \neq 0\) for all \(x \in [a,b]\)
Condition 2 — Same Sign
\(f'(a)\) and \(f'(b)\) must have the same sign
Condition 3 — Continuity
\(f\) is continuous and differentiable on \([a,b]\)
Initial Guess
Choose \(x_0 = \dfrac{a+b}{2}\) (midpoint of interval)
الشرط الأهم: المشتقة ما تصيرش صفر داخل الفترة، وكمان إشارة f′(a) تكون نفس إشارة f′(b).

Why same sign? If \(f'\) doesn't change sign, it can't be zero inside \([a,b]\) (by the Intermediate Value Theorem), guaranteeing the denominator never vanishes.

§ 04

Quadratic Convergence

Definition

Newton-Raphson is said to have quadratic (second-order) convergence. If \(e_n = |x^* - x_n|\) is the error at step \(n\), then:

\[ e_n \;\propto\; e_{n-1}^2 \qquad \Longleftrightarrow \qquad e_n = K \cdot e_{n-1}^2 \]

Each iteration squares the error — meaning correct decimal digits double each step.

التقارب التربيعي يعني: إذا عندك 3 أرقام صحيحة، بعد خطوة رح يصير عندك 6، وبعدها 12 — تتضاعف!
Formal Proof (Fixed-Point Framework)

View Newton's method as a fixed-point iteration \(x = g(x)\), where:

\[ g(x) \;=\; x - \frac{f(x)}{f'(x)} \]
Step 1 — Differentiate g(x)
g'(x) = 1 - [f'(x)·f'(x) - f(x)·f''(x)] / [f'(x)]²
\[ g'(x) \;=\; \frac{f(x)\cdot f''(x)}{\bigl[f'(x)\bigr]^2} \]
Step 2 — Evaluate at the root x*

At \(x^*\), we have \(f(x^*) = 0\), therefore:

\[ g'(x^*) \;=\; \frac{0 \cdot f''(x^*)}{\bigl[f'(x^*)\bigr]^2} \;=\; 0 \]
عند الجذر الحقيقي، مشتقة g تساوي صفر — وهاد هو مفتاح الإثبات.
Step 3 — Taylor Expansion

Expand \(g\) around \(x_{n-1}\) and evaluate at \(x^*\):

\[ g(x^*) \;=\; g(x_{n-1}) + (x^*-x_{n-1})\,g'(x_{n-1}) + \frac{(x^*-x_{n-1})^2}{2}\,g''(\xi) \]

Since \(g'(x^*) = 0\) and the first-order term vanishes:

\[ x^* - x_n \;=\; \frac{(x^*-x_{n-1})^2}{2}\,g''(\xi) \]
Conclusion
\[ \boxed{e_n \;=\; K\cdot e_{n-1}^2} \qquad K = \frac{g''(\xi)}{2} = \frac{f''(x^*)}{2\,f'(x^*)} \]

This completes the proof that Newton-Raphson has quadratic convergence near a simple root.

Solve \(x^3 - 2x^2 - 1 = 0\) on \([2,\,3]\) — 4 Iterations

Setup
\[ f(x) = x^3 - 2x^2 - 1, \qquad x \in [2,\,3] \] \[ f'(x) = 3x^2 - 4x \]
Condition Check

Verify \(f'(a)\) and \(f'(b)\) have the same sign:

\[ f'(2) = 3(2)^2 - 4(2) = 12 - 8 = 4 > 0 \quad (+) \] \[ f'(3) = 3(3)^2 - 4(3) = 27 - 12 = 15 > 0 \quad (+) \]
✓ f′(2) = 4 > 0 ✓ f′(3) = 15 > 0 ✓ Same sign → Method applicable
Iteration Formula
\[ x_n \;=\; x_{n-1} \;-\; \frac{x_{n-1}^3 - 2x_{n-1}^2 - 1}{3x_{n-1}^2 - 4x_{n-1}} \]

Initial guess: \(x_0 = \dfrac{2+3}{2} = 2.5\)
